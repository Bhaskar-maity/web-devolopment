Blackberry Sauce

  • Time:15 min
  • Serves:1.5 cups
Ingredients Quantity
1 pint blackberry (2 cups)
sugar 3 tablespoons)
lemon juice 1/4 teaspoon

Step 1: Puree the berries through a food mill or in a food processor and press them through a fine-mesh sieve to remove their seeds.

Step 2:Whisk in the sugar and lemon juice, whisking until the sugar dissolves.

Step 3:Taste for sweetness and adjust, if necessary.

Pitaya Adzuki Nice Cream Smoothie Bowl

  • Time:30 Mins
  • Serves:1 Bowl

Description:

Nice to eat cold; has an interesting texture if eaten at room temperature. (It's the vegan version of ice cream)

The base is usually frozen bananas. If you don't have frozen bananas, add ice into the blender.

I added psyllium husk to increase the soluble fibre content and to thicken the consistency. Same goes to the reason Avocado is added. Avocado is a good source of pantothenic acid, dietary fiber, vitamin K, copper, folate, vitamin B6, potassium, vitamin E, and vitamin C. However is high in calories, so use sparingly.

Nutrient Information
Energy: 500 kcal
Carbohydrates : 69g (2 servings)
Protein: 14g (1 serving)
Fats: 25g (5 servings)

Ingredents Quantity
Avocado 1/2
Medium Frozen Bananas 1
Vanilla Soy Isolate Protein Powder 3 TB
Large Red-Flesh Pitaya Frui 1/4
Adzuki beans (cooked) 1 TB
